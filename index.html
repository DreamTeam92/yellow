Swedish Massage

The primary goals of the Swedish massage technique is to relax the entire body. This is accomplished by rubbing the muscles with long gliding strokes. But Swedish massage therapy goes beyond relaxation.

Therapeutic Massage

Therapeutic massage is a general term that describes any type of massage modality that helps relieve pain, reduce stress, and work on a specific problem.

.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage is focuses on realigning deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. Great for chronic aches and pains and contracted areas.

Lymphatic Darainage

Lymphatic drainage is a therapeutic massage increase the flow of lymph and reduce toxins in your body. Lymph is key to keeping your immune system working properly, improving the flow and drainage is good for you in lots of ways.

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage is a catch-all term for any hands-on massage during or after pregnancy which has multiple positive effects i.e.: lowered anxiety, decreased back and leg pain, improved sleep, decreased levels of the stress hormone norepinephrine.

Efficiency over all

Hello, my name is Viktor. I am a massage therapist based in Chipping Norton and I am working at Soho Farmhouse. When I am not at work I offer mobile massage (at your home) services at your own convenience in this and the surrounding areas.

Viktor is trained and experienced Massage Therapist (Bamford Haybarn Spa, Soho Farmhouse Healthclub Spa, Grace Belgravia). He provide physical treatments for clients that relieve pain, reduce stress, increase relaxation, and improve general wellness.

He has a proven track record of helping clients to achieve peace and physical contentment through effective massage techniques. He is educated in various massage styles and techniques, involves very good anatomical knowledge and understanding bio mechanisms with a high standing client care. He is educating clients about rehabilitative and stretching exercises as well.

He works closely with Szabina - an exceptional Personal Trainer, Pilates Teacher and Corrective Exercise Specialist - for helping clients to get the best possible, long lasting results.
