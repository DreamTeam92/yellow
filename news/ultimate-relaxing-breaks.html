Daylesford Farm may have delicious food, covetable clothes and serious interiors inspo, but beauty aficionados such thing are purely sideshows. The star is Bamford Spa.

 

Beautifully designed, is uses heavenly organic products and has the tightest calibre of therapist. For this summer, it`s upped it`s offering with an outdoor spa. With all the chicness you would expect from a Daylesford project, there are three newly created "outdoor treatment suites". Basically posh tents, with gorgeous plants by the door, a central area to relax in, showers and loos. Plus, a private deck with hot tub, a table and loungers.

 

Each tent houses two treatment rooms, so the idea is you book with your friends, each paying £150, and get to hang out in luxury/privacy for three hours. You also get a Bamford massageor facial (ask for Victor, whose knot-busting skills are legendary). You can order food and drinks from the award-winning cafe (at extra cost), then quaff them at the table on your terrace, overlooking the orchard.

If you can stay longer there is also a meditation tent, with workshops and classes open to every guest (think yoga, twilight meditation and so on).

 

 

Bamford Haybarn Spa Summer Spa Experience, £150pp, two guests minimum; Bamford Wellness Spa Cotswold
